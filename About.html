Robert Powderly Guitar/Bass and SongWriter

Rob bio

Rob Grew up in the modest surroundings of Mucklon in Co.meath. He developed a keen interest in Metal Music at a young age, Rob would regulary attend school with his CD walkman listening to everything he could get his hands on in the Metal Genre. It was here where Rob started playing in local bands with his classmates where he began to play Bass. Soon after Rob developed a passion for Guitar and started playing in bands such as Black Martyr and Red Enemy. Rob has been a driving influence in Hero and Error who shows burning motivation and focused and serious approach to everything got to do with the band

Rob's main influences

  • Metallica
  • Bury your Dead
  • Architects
  • Deftones

Gary Regan Guitar/SongWriter

Gary Bio

Gary grew up in Lucan Co.Dublin and began his music career by taking up Guitar at the age of 13. Gary has toured in America with a band called Clergy and has experienced many impressive support slots with major bands like Mastodon. Gary has a very tradional style of Guitar playing mixed with a raw and aggressive technique. He is one of the founding members of Hero in Error and heavy influenced the writing process for the bands first EP "The High Points of New Lows"

Gary's main influences

  • Mastodon
  • Metallica
  • Thin Lizzy
  • Slayer
